A LITTLE BIT ABOUT ME

HI!

My name is Syafiqah Husna, but you can just call me Husna. I’m 22 years young, fresh and full of life (still not expired, don’t worry). I come from a small town called Gua Musang in Kelantan. Life here is simple—waking up to the sound of roosters, enjoying the green hills, and breathing in the fresh kampung air. Ahh, pure bliss! But don’t let the kampung girl vibes fool you. I’ve got a mission and a vision that’ll blow your socks off! People always say, “Small-town kids can’t dream big.” Well, guess what? I’m here to prove them wrong!


My Mission

My mission is simple: to be someone useful, not just for myself but for my family and my community. I want to inspire other small-town kids like me to chase their dreams. Who says we can’t be CEOs, inventors, or even world travelers? All we need is a little courage, a lot of hard work, and maybe some patience for the occasional "Can you even do it?" comments from skeptics.


My Vision

As for my vision, I want to see my kampung grow while keeping its unique charm. Imagine this: super-fast WiFi in the middle of rice fields, and kids from the kampung learning how to code while waiting for the cows to come home. Cool, right? I believe life is like cooking—get the right ingredients, the right recipe, and don’t turn the heat up too high. Slow and steady wins the race. If things don’t work out, just adjust the seasoning. Life’s the same; fall down, get back up, and keep moving forward. So, yeah, that’s me—Syafiqah Husna, a small-town girl with big dreams. Cheer for me, okay? Who knows, one day I might be a big shot, and you’ll say, “I read her story once!”
